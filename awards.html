Awards / Major Production Accomplishments

MAKING WAVES (2011) - produced and directed by Frances McElroy; supported in part with a 2009 Pew Fellowship in the Arts and a 2010 Transformation Award from the Leeway Foundation. Other supporters include The William Penn Foundation, Independence Foundation, Pennsylvania Partners in the Arts, Pennsylvania Humanities Council, Philadelphia Independent Film & Video Association.

MIRROR DANCE - co-produced and co-directed by Frances McElroy and Maria Rodriguez; CINE Golden Eagle Award, 2005; First Place for Documentary Excellence, Society of Professional Journalists, Greater Philadelphia Chapter; 2006 Imagen Award finalist for Best Documentary for Television or Film; Award of Merit, Latin American Studies Association Film Festival, Montreal, Canada, 2007; awarded major support from ITVS (Independent Television Service/CPB). Other supporters include Latino Public Broadcasting, the National Endowment for the Arts, The Philadelphia Foundation, the 5-County Arts Fund, the Montgomery County Foundation, and the Philadelphia Independent Film & Video Association. The documentary premiered nationally in 2005 on the Emmy Award winning PBS series INDEPENDENT LENS. It is now seen on the PBS series GLOBAL VOICES.
